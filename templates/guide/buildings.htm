Buildings

Apart from Units, buildings is the other major portion of your economy. But make sure that you chose the buildings that you wish to build wisely as most of them require a lot of resources, make sure you produce enough of that resource before you build. Here is the list of buildings available to you, all buildings are 8 networth each.

Note: Some buildings have a tech requirement, the tech requirement stated is not to be able to build the building but to be able to build it without penalty.
