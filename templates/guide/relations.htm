Relations

Below is an example of the relations page you may see. A yellow image means that one of the sides has offered some form of peace, a NAP (Non-Aggresion Pact), CF (Ceasefire) or Ally/ Alliance. Green is when one of these offers of peace has been accepted by both sides. Red is War and as such, only needs to be declared by either party.

As Prime Minister of your Empire, if a NAP or CF has been offered, you will have the option to accept it from this page. Likewise, as Prime Minister, you will be given the option to cancel any NAPs/ Alliances

Notice: With the game still in some form of development and a lower player base, alliances are yet to be completed.
