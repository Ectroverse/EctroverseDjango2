Energy

Production: The total amount of Energy your Solar Collectors and Fission Reactor produce.

Decay: The amount of energy you lose due to decay...the more energy you have the greater your decay...your decay cannot exceed your production unless you buy the energy on the market.

Building Upkeep: The total amount of energy you use up every turn to pay for your buildings.

Unit Upkeep: The total amount of energy you use up every turn to pay for your units.

Population upkeep reduction: Your population reduces the buildings upkeep, read more about it here.

 

Resources

Tells you how much of each resource you produce. The decay works the same way for crystals as energy. Minerals are produced my Mineral Plants, Crystals by Crystal Laboratories and Ectrolium by Refinement Stations. 

 

Buildings and Units:

Tells you the amount of buildings and units you currently own as well as the ones that you have under construction.
