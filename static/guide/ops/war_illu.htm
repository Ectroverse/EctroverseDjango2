War Illusion
Creates various illusions of ships and units for the targeted empire, useful to draw fire away from friendly units and encourage enemy troops to flee, cumulative

Week 26, year 0
Your psychics are casting War Illusions on your empire.
Various illusions will accompany your fleets for 35 weeks, keeping around 10 % of enemy fire away from friendly units.

(Note: results may vary depending on the size of your empire and your number of psychics)
