Here is a simple market page, here you can buy and sell resources for energy. There are 4 major pieces of information on the market screen. First you can see the buy price for a recourse. This is the price the resource is wanted at, and you can also see how much of it the buyer wants. There is also the sell price and that is the amount of energy the seller wants to receive for each unit of his resource and how much of it he wants to sell. Here you can only see the bid at the current price. Press on market statistics to find out about other bids on the market close to the current buy/sell price..
