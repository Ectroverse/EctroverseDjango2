This is the ultimate ground killing machine. Equipped with dual Banshee machine guns and ultra-long range Hellfire missiles it can kill pretty much anything that moves. Involved in the 3rd and 4th stages of combat Goliaths shoot their missiles into orbit trying to hit enemy bombers and cruisers as well as any transport heading towards the planet's surface to land troops. In the last stage it acts as a ground-to-ground unit. Over twice as powerful as any other ground unit it can easily kill anything, but the power comes at a great price and there is always a risk of being simply overrun by cheaper units.

AA: Attack against enemy air units
AD: Defense from enemy air units
GA: Attack against enemy Goliaths
GD: Defense from enemy anti-air ground units
