Rumors are that these units appear out of thin air on the battlefield, and many say that they didn't pay attention to them thinking it was just an illusion to distract them until Phantoms started doing some real damage. Few people that have encountered these creatures survived the encounter to tell the story. The weapons used by these...things...outclass even those of the goliath, and their armor appears to be almost impenetrable by conventional weapons. Only one race however, the Manticarias can wield these destructive ground units. The drawback of the Phantom is the fact that they decay at a rate of 1% per tick.
