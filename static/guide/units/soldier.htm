Soldier is your basic ground unit, cheap and quick to train these soldiers can be a great fighting force. The main drawback is that they need to be landed on the planet by transports which can be easily shot down. There are also more technologically advanced units which can easily wipe out soldiers. If you wish to use soldiers as backbone of your ground fleet you need to make sure that you have a lot more of them than your opponent has of anything else.

AA: Attack against enemy air units
AD: Defense from enemy air units
GA: Attack against enemy Goliaths
GD: Defense from enemy anti-air ground units
