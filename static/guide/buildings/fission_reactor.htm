  Fission Reactor

>>Base cost<<
450 Energy, 20 Minerals, 12 Crystals, 8 Ectrolium

>>Construction Time<<
14 standard weeks

>>Upkeep<<
20 Energy per standard week

>>Networth<<
8 points

>>Tech Required<<
100%

Rather than Collect Solar energy these huge planets produce energy by making Uranium 238 undergo a controlled nuclear fission. Each reactor produces up to 40 energy per week when operating at full capacity and takes up no more space than a solar collector. The main disadvantage of the reactor is the complexity of technology involved in building and sustaining it. An empire needs to have a significant technology research to build these monster reactors.
