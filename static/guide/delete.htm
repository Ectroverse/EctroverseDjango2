Deletion

The name all speaks for itself, this is the place where you can delete your faction, but there are a number of choices between which you can choose:

1.Delete the faction and the account
2.Keep the account to join a different family

You can also choose a reason for which you are deleting, that is probably one of the funniest parts of the game:

1.I retired and lived happily ever after.
2.I slipped on a soap bar.
3.I mixed the television and the auto-destruction device remotes.
4.I got into an argument with my boa constrictor pet.
5.I caught my tie in the blender.
6.A chicken bone got stuck in my throat.
