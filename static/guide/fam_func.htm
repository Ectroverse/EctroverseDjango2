Empire Forum

This is a place where you can exchange news and latest developments with the rest of your empire. Everyone in the empire can post and read in this forum, but no outsiders can get in.

 

Change Vote

Here you can select you leader. In order for someone to become a leader he needs 1 vote more than the next person, or if they are tied the leader is decided by the latest vote.

 

Send Aid

Here you can send aid to other empire members, just select the faction you wish to aid from the drop-down list and type the amount of resources you wish to send 

 

Empire news

Tells you what happened in the empire in the past year, it shows things like who was attacked and who attacked someone else, as well as who explored what planets and who sent aid, you'll understand it better once you first see it.
