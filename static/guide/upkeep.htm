Upkeep

Upkeep is the amount of money you need to pay each turn in order to keep you buildings and units, if you do not pay the full amount of upkeep that is required, then your units will start to desert and you fleet readiness will fall, and your buildings will start to fall into ruins, here is a list of buildings and units and amount of upkeep which each of them needs:
